Hi there! I'm Nikki.

Before I was a nutritional
therapy practitioner, no one was listening to me.

At 35, I was being bounced between doctors with no real answers as to why I was having night sweats. I was healthy. I worked out every day — but I could never lose weight. I thought this was part of aging.

Frustrated, I went back to school for nutrition and learned a simple, yet astonishing thing: if you correct the root of the issue you can correct the problem.

One by one, I fixed my own issues: thyroid, weight loss resistance, food intolerances, fatigue. I quit my corporate job and started helping others do the same.

My approach is different, and that's why it works. I consider the whole person and recommend a path that makes sense for you. It's not just nutrition!
