Six Principles of Conscious Design

The Digital Design Deck

Six truths about what your website is actually doing, drawn from the same energetic principles that guide the practices of the clients we serve.

DMWN
01  ·  DIGITAL DESIGN DECK

Your website is an energetic portal

Every person who lands on your site enters your field before they ever meet you. Slow load times, cluttered layouts, and broken links are energetic leaks. Your digital space deserves the same intentional curation as your healing room.

Portal
DMWN
02  ·  DIGITAL DESIGN DECK

First impressions are felt, not read

Your visitors decide within 3 seconds whether they feel safe enough to stay. That feeling comes from color, space, and imagery. Not your words. Design your homepage the way you would set a sacred space.

Presence
DMWN
03  ·  DIGITAL DESIGN DECK

Fonts carry frequency

A harsh corporate font on a sound healing page creates dissonance before a word is read. Typography has its own energy. The fonts on your site should feel like an extension of your practice: soft, intentional, and aligned.

Vibration
DMWN
04  ·  DIGITAL DESIGN DECK

White space is not empty space

In healing work you know that silence holds as much power as sound. Crowding your site out of fear of emptiness blocks the energy of what you're offering. Let your site breathe.

Spaciousness
DMWN
05  ·  DIGITAL DESIGN DECK

Your colors speak when you aren't

Color is vibration. Every color on your site is communicating something to your visitor's nervous system before their conscious mind catches up. Choose your palette like you choose what to wear to ceremony.

Transmission
DMWN
06  ·  DIGITAL DESIGN DECK

Stock photos break the spell

Generic smiling stock images are the digital equivalent of synthetic crystals. Your visitors can feel the inauthenticity. Real photos of you, your space, and your actual work create resonance.

Conscious Web Design
The Craftsman

Will Jones

"I'm not just building your website. I'm transmitting your field into the digital realm."

Will has always been two things: creative and spiritual. He earned his B.A. in Communications studying web design, and from the beginning, digital craft was his medium of expression, not just his vocation.

In 2022, he followed a drawing towards energy healing he'd felt all of his life and got his Usui Holy Fire Reiki I & II Certification. What he found in that training changed how he sees everything he creates. The principles of energy work — presence, intention, frequency, space — aren't only for the healing room. They translate directly into how a website feels. He hasn't designed the same way since.

The Reiki Journey The International Center for Reiki Training  ·  May 15, 2022

The attunements gave him new eyes. He saw that the principles guiding energy healing are the same principles that make a website feel alive or feel dead. Intention. Presence. Spaciousness. Resonance. The layout, the color temperature, the white space, the rhythm of the scroll: all of it is felt before it is read. That understanding now lives in every site he builds.

When he works with energy workers, healers, shamanic coaches, and sacred feminine practitioners, he brings something most web designers can't: the experience of having sat at the same table, studied the same symbols, and felt the same current.

Every deck, every text, every practice has shaped the way he works.
