About Us

Omaha's Dedicated
Simulator Specialists

We're not a big-box retailer shipping you a kit. Omaha Custom Sims is a locally owned design and installation company focused exclusively on premium indoor golf simulators. We serve Omaha, Elkhorn, Papillion, Bellevue, and the surrounding metro.

Every project begins with an in-home consultation. We assess your space, discuss your goals, and design a system that fits your room, your game, and your budget — from basement builds to dedicated sim rooms and commercial setups.
