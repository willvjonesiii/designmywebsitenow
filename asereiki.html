What is Reiki?

Reiki is one of the many forms of ancient energy healing, originating in Japan in the early 20th century. It works with the unseen life force energy flowing through our bodies — Rei meaning universal, Ki meaning life force energy.

In a session, you lie fully clothed while the practitioner gently moves their hands just above or directly on the body, holding each placement for 3–5 minutes — clearing blockages, restoring flow, and inviting deep healing. There are no negative side effects. Reiki can only be used for good.

Meet the Healer

A calling she was
born to answer.

From feeling different as a child to becoming a certified Reiki practitioner and healer, Kenya has grown into her divine gift. Her deep empathy towards all living things has guided her in many situations — helping her live a life of purpose.

In 2018, struggling through a difficult chapter, she began a journey to find herself. That journey led her to Reiki. After completing Reiki I certification and later Reiki II in 2022, she also explored herbalism — creating tinctures, oils, and salves that became beloved in her community.

After doing deep inner and shadow work, Kenya launched Asé Reiki full-time. Her long-distance goal became close and real. "I have identified the love and light that possesses me."
