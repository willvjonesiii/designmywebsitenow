Who We Are

Your neighborhood dental family in Las Cruces.

At Organ Ocean Dental, we believe going to the dentist should feel comfortable — not clinical. Our team of caring professionals has been serving the Las Cruces community with warmth and expertise for over two decades.

From your child's first checkup to full-mouth restorations, every patient receives personalized attention and honest recommendations. We speak your language — literally. Our bilingual staff ensures every patient feels heard.
