About In One Heart

Uniting souls on the
path of remembrance

In One Heart was founded by Evelyn Viviana Guerra with the vision to unite people of all walks of life and assist them on their spiritual journeys and to embark on their path of remembering their missions in life with love and authenticity.

The In One Heart Tribe offers a wide range of workshops, one on one guidance and Mystical Journeys so that each individual can embody and experience their own potential and unique spiritual healing, letting go of what no longer serves them and embodying their authentic selves.

About Evelyn Viviana

A rare teacher of
shamanic wisdom

Evelyn is a renowned life coach, healer, and spiritual light energy worker born in Peru and driven by the calling of inner light in her spirit. She is committed to her own awakening as well as to the calling of Spirit to help humanity awaken to their own powers and inner wisdom, one person at a time.

Evelyn is a certified teacher of The Awakening the Illuminated Heart (ATIH) and has completed dozens of Vision Quests with guardians of Indigenous knowledge as a seeker and student of spiritual truths for healing, and psychic transformation. She is a leader in imparting shamanic wisdom and esoteric knowledges, much of which are found in Indigenous traditions, through her decades of teaching and leading retreats, meditations, shamanic breath-work, workshops, spiritual and mystic journeys to sacred sites throughout the world. She now works with a dynamic team of shamanic light workers, spiritual teachers, healers, and humble servants of the Divine Spirit to expand on the journeys of many spiritual seekers.

She is a direct student of the world-renowned author and esoteric teacher Drunvalo Melchizedek, and is a certified student of his "School of Remembering." She credits the honor of learning directly from him to gaining a deeper understanding of her synergistic gifts and abilities of imparting the teachings learned in her own life's spiritual journey onto her students.

Evelyn is a highly intuitive and sensitive eternal student of the light, who has become finely attuned to the voice of inner wisdom and of Spirit. She is a rare teacher of shamanic wisdom and the art of esoteric knowledge. Her greatest delight is in being a witness to the growth of her students as they remember who they really are and embrace their path of ultimate fulfillment in experiencing the divine rapture found in Spirit, life, and love.

Evelyn has been blessed to embrace, learn and hold sacred space with various indigenous tribes from the Shipibo's and Q'eros in Peru to The Mayans of Guatemala & Mexico to the Maori's of New Zealand and Kogis of Colombia as well as bring the science aspect of this reality by teaching Sacred Geometry speak on Quantum Physics & Quantum Entanglement and marrying all she's learned and experienced with religious sects like Buddhism, Hinduism, and Sufism. She embraces these subjects into her practice everyday ultimately innerstanding the beauty of this reality and embracing it all with love, authenticity and humility.
