Clinical Philosophy — Luminous Therapeutics
01

Clinical Intake & Assessment

Comprehensive health history, postural assessment, range-of-motion testing, and goal setting before treatment begins.

02

Individualized Treatment Plan

A written plan detailing modalities, session frequency, measurable goals, and expected milestones for your condition.

03

Tracked Progress & Documentation

Session notes, outcome scores, and progress reports available for you and your referring physician at every stage.

04

Coordinated Physician Communication

We work alongside your care team — sharing notes and adjusting treatment as your condition and provider feedback evolves.

Conditions We Treat

If conventional care
hasn't been enough —
we specialize in complex.

Pain Management

Chronic Pain Syndromes

Fibromyalgia, myofascial pain syndrome, tension-type headache, and persistent musculoskeletal pain unresolved by other treatments.

Injury Recovery

Soft Tissue & Joint Injuries

Whiplash, rotator cuff injuries, plantar fasciitis, IT band syndrome, and repetitive strain conditions.

Lymphatic

Lymphedema & Edema

Primary and secondary lymphedema, post-mastectomy arm swelling, lipoedema, and chronic lower extremity edema.

Surgical Recovery

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Following joint replacement, cosmetic surgery, mastectomy, abdominal procedures, and orthopedic surgery.

Oncology

Oncology Massage & Support

Gentle, evidence-informed massage for patients during and after cancer treatment — fatigue, neuropathy, and lymphedema management.

Maternal Health

Prenatal & Postpartum Care

Clinical massage for pregnancy-related pain, postpartum recovery, C-section scar tissue, and diastasis-related tension.
