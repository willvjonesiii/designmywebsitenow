4 hours of coverage. Perfect for intimate ceremonies, courthouse weddings, or couples who just want the essential moments without the full day production.

8–10 hours from getting ready to last dance. Full documentary coverage — every chaotic, beautiful, unscripted moment your day actually is.

Why film?
Because digital
is too perfect.

I love all things vintage. 35mm film has a quality that can't be replicated — the grain, the warmth, the slight imperfections. Real love doesn't look like a retouched ad campaign. Film doesn't either.
